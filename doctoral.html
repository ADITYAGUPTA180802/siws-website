About Doctoral Programs

The Doctoral (Ph.D.) Programs at SIWS College are aimed at fostering advanced research, innovation, and intellectual growth. These programs are designed for scholars who wish to explore, analyze, and contribute new insights to their academic disciplines under expert guidance.

The college encourages interdisciplinary research and supports candidates in developing a deep understanding of their subjects while addressing real-world challenges. Doctoral research scholars work under the supervision of experienced faculty and collaborate with industries and research organizations.

Available Doctoral Research Areas

Research Area Department Duration
Commerce and Business Studies Department of Commerce 3–5 Years
Information Technology and Data Science Department of Computer Science 3–5 Years
Environmental and Chemical Sciences Department of Chemistry 3–5 Years
Management and Financial Research Department of Management Studies 3–5 Years

Eligibility Criteria

  • Master’s Degree in the relevant field with at least 55% marks (or equivalent grade).
  • Qualifying entrance test conducted by University of Mumbai or SIWS College (as applicable).
  • Submission of a detailed Research Proposal approved by the Research Committee.

SIWS College ensures that each doctoral candidate receives strong mentorship, access to modern research facilities, and opportunities for publishing in reputed journals. The research culture at SIWS promotes ethics, innovation, and community-oriented outcomes.
