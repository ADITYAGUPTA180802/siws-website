Laboratory

The third floor of the main building of college houses aerated, well-lit and spacious science Laboratories;
which are instrumental in comprehending concepts through practical learning.

There are laboratories for each subject – Physics, Chemistry and Microbiology.

The Physics Laboratory is equipped with all the instruments needed to acquaint the working principles and mechanisms of
the Physics concepts to the students.

Laboratory also boasts of a dark room to assimilate the optical experiments The Laboratory of Chemistry is embellished with vivid bottles of Chemicals and
other requirements attaining to analytical, organic and inorganic trials.

Microbiology Laboratory comprises of Molecular and Bioinformatics Laboratory and is housed with necessary instruments;
the refrigerator encases the living culture and specimens.
A spacious Research Laboratory is facilitated for the faculty and students with modern amenities to conduct dry and wet research.
