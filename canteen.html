Canteen

Situated in the rear of the college main building, a suitable place for grapevine communication; serving folks with aromatic beverages and appetizing brunch.

The institute pertains its tradition not only through the infrastructure, academics, staffs and students but also in the healthy and hygienic food served by the canteen.

The Canteen also serves delicious and sumptuous South Indian varieties including sweets at various ceremonies.
