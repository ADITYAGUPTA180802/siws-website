Health Care Center

The College has a Medical centre where a Doctor comes twice a week and provides free health check up for staff and students.
The doctor is available on call to visit the centre during emergency.

For the physical well-being of the students and staffs, the institute has laid out a health center.

Located in the ground floor of the college main building, the health center is equipped with basic medical provision viz. bed, stretcher and wheelchair.

The room is facilitated with the emergency medical kit with prescribed medicines, and the required facilities in case of any emergency.

An on call doctor is available for visit to the center to react to emergencies

In case of major cases, students are shifted to hospitals nearby and the students’ parents are informed.

The center also conducts health check-ups by making a tie up with nearby hospital.
