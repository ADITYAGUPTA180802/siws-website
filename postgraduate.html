About Postgraduate Programs

SIWS College offers Postgraduate programs that aim to promote higher education, research aptitude, and professional excellence. These programs are designed to provide students with advanced academic knowledge and skill development in their chosen fields, preparing them for leadership roles and doctoral research.

Available Postgraduate Courses

Program Duration Eligibility
Master of Commerce (M.Com) 2 Years B.Com or equivalent degree from a recognized university
Master of Science in Information Technology (M.Sc. IT) 2 Years B.Sc. IT / B.Sc. Computer Science / BCA or equivalent
Master of Science (M.Sc. Chemistry) 2 Years B.Sc. with Chemistry as a major subject
Master of Management Studies (MMS) 2 Years Graduation in any stream with minimum 50% marks (Entrance-based)

These programs are structured to combine academic theory with practical learning through projects, seminars, and industry collaborations. SIWS College ensures that every postgraduate student develops strong analytical and professional skills for successful careers ahead.
